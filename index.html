I’m a Cognitive Psychology PhD student in The Reasoning Lab and Computational Vision and Learning Lab at UCLA. My advisors are Keith Holyoak and Hongjing Lu. I integrate behavioral experiments, computational and cognitive models, and large naturalistic studies to study beliefs through the lens of social cognition. Projects I’m currently working on focus on the formation and correction of misconceptions, the spread of misinformation online, and the structure of people’s belief systems surrounding social and scientific issues (such as climate change, vaccine safety, and transgender rights). Personally, I love warm weather and good music more than maybe anything. My twitter is @HunterPriniski.
