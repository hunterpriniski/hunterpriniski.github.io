i am a cognitive psych/computational cognition phd student in the reasoning lab at ucla (advised by keith holyoak and hongjing lu). my research uses behavioral experiments, computational modeling, and analysis of naturalistic data to better understand a. the social + cognitive processes by which misconceptions are adopted and spread online and b. how to correct emerging misconceptions about science and one's society. personally, i love warm weather and good music more than maybe anything. twitter: @HunterPriniski.
