I'm Hunter Priniski and I'll be starting a PhD in Cognitive Psychology this Fall at UCLA. I will be advised by Keith Holyoak and Hongjing Lu. My research interests involve using behavioral experiments, computational cognitive models, and naturalistic studies to investigate the roles information and evidence play in the formation of both strongly-held (such as moral or political) and everyday beliefs. I also study topics in belief change and develop theory-driven interventions that promote social good.
