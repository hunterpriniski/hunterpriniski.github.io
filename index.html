I'm a Cognitive Psychology PhD student at UCLA. My advisors are Keith Holyoak and Hongjing Lu. My research uses behavioral experiments, computational models, and naturalistic studies to investigate the roles information and evidence play in the formation of strongly held beliefs (such as political or moral beliefs). I also study topics in belief change and develop theory driven interventions to promote social good.
