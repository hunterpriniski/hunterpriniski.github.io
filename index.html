I'm a first year Computational Cognition PhD student at UCLA, advised by Keith Holyoak and Hongjing Lu. My research brings together experimental work with cognitive modeling and naturalistic studies to study how people's often complex belief systems surrounding social and scientific issues are strucutred. I am also working on a project that leverages crowdsourcing and Big Data to facilate the development of educational interventions that can change people's misconceptions.

I was born and raised in Arizona and got my BS in pure math at Arizona State University. I enjoy playing and listening to music, working out, and hanging on the beach with friends.
