I'm Hunter Priniski and I'm a research assistant in the Cognition, Computation, and Development Lab at Arizona State University. My research interests involve using behavioral experiments and naturalistic studies to investigate the roles information and evidence play in the formation of strongly-held beliefs (such as moral or political beliefs). I also study topics in belief change and develop theory-driven interventions that promote social good.
