I'm a Computational Cognition PhD student in the Department of Psychology at UCLA, advised by Keith Holyoak and Hongjing Lu. In my research I use behavioral experiments, computational models, and naturalistic studies to investigate the roles information and evidence play in the formation of strongly held beliefs (such as political or moral beliefs). I also study topics in belief change and develop theory driven interventions to promote social good.
