about me.

I do data + cognitive science for the Cognition, Computation, and Development lab at Arizona State University. I will be applying for PhD programs in cognitive psychology this upcoming fall.

Currently, much of my research focuses on how people form and change their beliefs. Specifically, I'm looking at how Reddit's Change My View can elucidate the mechanisms of attitude change. But in the past, I've spent some time investigating collective problem solving in the darkweb and using machine learning to reduce sampling bias in large Twitter datasets.
